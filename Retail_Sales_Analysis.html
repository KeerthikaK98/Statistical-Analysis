Introduction: This project involves analyzing a dataset related to Sales of products in the year of 2020. When it comes to retail business or any kind of business, Sales plays a major role in deciding the business growth. Analytics help businesses make informed decisions by analyzing various parameters. Analytics has been used in various domains of retail including supply chain, inventory, manufacturing, marketing, fraud detection etc. Our project focuses on Sales performance with metrics like Market, Region, Segment, Division, Date, Shipping mode, Order priority and performance metrics like product price, shipping cost, quantity ordered, returned quantity, loss per return. There are various questions that can be answered with these metrics including sales across months, profit across markets, Top 5 or Top 10 best performing products with high sales and profit, Ship mode that is preferred in each region and the cost corresponding to it, Returns across countries and which product has been returned the most etc.

Descriptive statistics involve collecting, organizing, summarizing and presenting the data (Bluman,2023). Descriptive Statistics is interpreted using measures of average, measures of variation and measures of position. It has been used in businesses for customer segmentation, sales performance, market trends etc.

Generalizing from samples to populations, estimating and testing hypotheses, identifying correlations between variables, and formulating predictions are all components of inferential statistics (Bluman,2023). Inferential statistics uses probability. It is being used in sales forecasting, market research, quality control with the help of regression analysis, hypothesis testing, sampling, confidence intervals etc.

R script is used for writing and executing code or any data related task where report is not needed. On the other hand R Markdown can contain code, analysis and text in a document format that can be shared and reproduced. R script is ideal for data analysis, building models etc, while R Markdown is ideal for creating detailed reports.
